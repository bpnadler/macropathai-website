Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a meal?

You can log a meal by typing, speaking, or taking a photo of your food. MacroPath AI will automatically calculate the macros and calories for you.

How do I change my nutrition goals?

Go to the Settings tab in the app and tap "Nutrition Goals" to update your daily calorie and macro targets.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, then tap "Subscriptions" to manage or cancel your MacroPath AI subscription.

My data isn't syncing. What should I do?

Make sure you are connected to the internet and signed in with your Apple ID. If the issue persists, try closing and reopening the app. Contact us at support@macropathai.coma> if the problem continues.
