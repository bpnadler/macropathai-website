Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

Introduction

MacroPath AI ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the MacroPath AI mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Apple ID email address, display name, and Apple-provided user identifier (via Sign in with Apple)
  • Nutrition Data: Meals logged, food items, macro/calorie data, nutrition goals, and saved favorites
  • Photo Data: Food photos submitted for AI analysis (processed in real-time, stored only if you save the meal)
  • Voice Data: Voice recordings are temporarily processed for meal logging and are NOT stored permanently
  • Usage Data: API usage statistics, error logs, and feature usage analytics
  • Device Information: iPhone model, iOS version, and app version
  • Subscription Data: Subscription status, purchase history, and reward tier progress (managed via RevenueCat)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered nutrition tracking and food logging services
  • Process voice commands, text input, and food photos to identify meals and calculate nutrition data
  • Help you set, track, and achieve nutrition goals
  • Manage your account, subscription, and reward tier status
  • Improve AI accuracy, app performance, and user experience
  • Send notifications about trial status, reward milestones, and subscription updates

Data Sources

Nutrition data is powered by the USDA FoodData Central database (public domain), containing 61,000+ foods. Additional sources include restaurant nutrition information and manufacturer-provided data.

Source: U.S. Department of Agriculture, Agricultural Research Service. FoodData Central, 2024. fdc.nal.usda.gov

Data Security

  • All data encrypted at rest and in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row Level Security (RLS) ensures users can only access their own data
  • API rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Secure Apple Sign In authentication (no passwords stored)
  • Regular security audits of database access policies

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and export your nutrition data at any time (JSON format available in-app)
  • Edit your meals, food items, goals, and account information
  • Delete your account and ALL associated data permanently (irreversible)
  • Opt out of photo-based tracking and use voice/text entry only

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

If you are a resident of the European Union or California, you have additional rights including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to not be discriminated against for exercising your rights (CCPA)

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@macropathai.com.

Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to providing MacroPath AI services
  • Use your nutrition data to train AI models

Data is only shared with the service providers listed above, solely to provide MacroPath AI functionality.

Children's Privacy

MacroPath AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us data, contact us immediately at support@macropathai.com.

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Voice recordings: Processed in real-time and discarded immediately (not stored)
  • Photo data: Stored only if you confirm and save the meal; otherwise discarded after processing

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notification. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
